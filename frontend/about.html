How Our AI Works

SmartHire AI evaluates candidates by aligning resumes with HR-defined job requirements using Natural Language Processing techniques.

  • TF-IDF + Cosine Similarity for relevance
  • Skill-based matching
  • Experience alignment
  • Explainable keyword-level insights

Ethical AI

The system does not replace HR decisions. It provides transparent, explainable insights to support fair and unbiased hiring.
